- dovecot https://doc.dovecot.org/configuration_manual/protocols/lda/
- man https://linux.die.net/man/1/dovecot-lda
  - The dovecot-lda is a LDA which takes mail from an MTA
    and delivers it to a user's mailbox,
    while keeping Dovecot index files up to date.
  - features
    1) indexing though delivery
    2) quota enforcing plugin
    3) sieve support through pigeonhole plugin
